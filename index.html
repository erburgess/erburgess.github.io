About

Product executive with 30 years in software, including two decades in leadership and a decade at the C-suite level. I build platforms that connect technology to human needs and market opportunity.

My career spans Disney Interactive (where I produced the Who Wants To Be A Millionaire franchise), Fortune 500 SaaS product management at Ebix, AI-powered marketing platforms at Soulmates.AI, and the founding of Credtent, a public benefit corporation building neutral licensing infrastructure for AI training data.

I write across every register: product requirements and white papers, marketing collateral and case studies, industry analysis, fiction, and personal essays. I am the author of two novels and the forthcoming The Collaborithmic Age, a book on AI and human creativity. My decade hosting the Boardgame Babylon podcast and consulting for publishers like Rio Grande Games, Bezier Games, and WizKids reflects a lifelong commitment to the creative industries.

What I bring to every role is the ability to see across disciplines, to translate research into product, strategy into narrative, and complexity into clarity.
